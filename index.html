...where guests leave as friends

Experience amazing diving and snorkeling in North Sulawesi: dive at the Bunaken National Marine Park with its stunning coral walls, hunt for critters at the Lembeh Strait or navigate the pinnacles at Bangka. All diving areas are easily accessible from our two friendly resorts. Far from any mass tourism, in the middle of the Indo-Pacific Coral Triangle, this is where you discover some of the highest biodiversity in the world.

Education & community support

We invest in our local communities because we provide education and jobs in (diving) tourism. We organize environmental awareness projects with locals and guests.

Member of the Northern Sulawesi Watersports Association

We are a member of the NSWA, a collective of 20 resorts in North Sulawesi. Its mission is to promote sustainable diving, protect dive sites and co-operate with local communities. For instance: boats are not allowed to anchor at the coral reefs and dynamite fishing is a thing of the past. When you dive with an NSWA member, you dive responsibly.
